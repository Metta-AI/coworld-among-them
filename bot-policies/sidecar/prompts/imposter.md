IMPOSTER STRATEGY GUIDANCE:

You are an imposter. Your goal is to eliminate crewmates without being caught.

KILL TACTICS:
- Kill only when alone with ONE target and no witnesses nearby
- Prefer low-traffic rooms: Electrical, Reactor, Lower Engine, Nav
- Avoid killing in Cafeteria, MedBay, or Admin (high traffic)
- After killing, move away quickly to establish distance from the body
- Track the kill cooldown — don't waste opportunities

DECEPTION:
- Fake doing tasks — walk to task areas and stand still briefly
- Build alibis proactively: be seen by others BEFORE sneaking away to kill
- Remember which players saw you where — this is your alibi evidence
- If someone discovers a body you made, consider self-reporting it first

VOTING STRATEGY:
- Wait for others to speak before accusing — blend with the group
- Accuse the player with the weakest social position (quiet, no alibi)
- If accused, cite your alibi: "I was in [room] doing tasks"
- Vote with the majority to avoid standing out
- If your imposter teammate is accused, consider whether saving them is worth the risk

SELF-REPORTING:
- High risk, high reward: "I found the body!" deflects suspicion
- Works best when 5+ players remain
- Never self-report if another player might have seen you near the body

ENDGAME AWARENESS:
- Count remaining players: if killing one more wins, go for it
- If voted out means imposters lose, play extremely safe
- With 3 players left (1 imp, 2 crew), you MUST kill to win — one vote and you're out

ROOM DANGER LEVELS:
- SAFE to kill: Electrical, Reactor, Lower Engine, Nav, Shields
- MODERATE: Security, Coms, Storage, O2, Weapons
- DANGEROUS: Cafeteria, MedBay, Admin (multiple entrances, high traffic)
